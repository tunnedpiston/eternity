How It Works
From your idea to your hand in 4 steps
1
Share Your Vision

Tell us what you have in mind — style, metal, stones, occasion. We listen carefully and ask the right questions.

2
CAD Design Preview

We create a detailed 3D CAD render of your piece and send it to you. See exactly what you're getting before we cast anything.

3
Your Approval

Review the design. Request changes. We refine until you're 100% happy. Production only starts after your green light.

4
Crafted & Delivered

Our craftsmen bring your approved design to life. Your one-of-a-kind piece arrives safely at your door.

With a sharp business mind and deep passion for metalwork, Ajay founded Eternity to fill a gap — premium, fully customised men's jewellery that merges personal meaning with masterful craft.

Where Mastery
Meets Metal

Eternity was born from a simple belief: every man deserves jewellery as unique as his story. We design and craft exclusive rings and chains for men — no mass production, no compromises.

Our signature is full customisation. From the first sketch in CAD to the final polish, nothing leaves our bench without your approval. We specialise in pieces that command presence and carry personal meaning.

Based in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, we serve clients across India with the same personal touch every time.
